Bathymetry Graph

  1. Click 'Data entry template', and make a copy of the template spreadsheet.
  2. Fill in your data.
  3. Go to share options and change the sharing settings to 'Anyone with the link.'
  4. Copy the URL for your spreadsheet.
  5. Paste it in the 'Spreadsheet URL' field below. Click 'Generate Graph.'

Note: if your graph isn't loading, check to make sure you set the sharing settings to 'Anyone with the link.'
